Reflections on JS

Course Paper

In this work we intend to present a c review of the literature on the subject of gamification applied to the area of education, demonstrating in these studies examples of gamification techniques in elementary schools in Latin America, Spain and Portugal. It was applied appropriate techniques of literature review: specific search strategies that aim to detect as much relevant literature as possible, documented search so that readers can evaluate rigor and completeness; explicit inclusion and exclusion criteria to evaluate each of the literature and the presentation of the results of the study by means of graphs and tables, together with the discussions
