Reflections on JS

Course Paper

In this work I intended to present a review of the literature on the subject of gamification applied to the area of education, demonstrating in these studies examples of gamification techniques in elementary schools in Latin America, Spain and Portugal. It was applied appropriate techniques of literature review: specific search strategies that aim to detect as much relevant literature as possible, documented search so that readers can evaluate rigor and completeness; explicit inclusion and exclusion criteria to evaluate each of the literature and the presentation of the results of the study by means of graphs and tables, together with the discussions.

Takeaways

However, it seems an extremely optmist view of education which is complex (teachers' trainning, social/political/cultural context, etc.) their schools or they believe that it may represent a workload in their work.

  • ✿ The importance of making explicit elements of the scientific method for data collection. In this case, the collection of articles on gamification applied in elementary schools in the University of Gothenburg database.
  • ✿ The writing of an academic text has certain particularities, such as the presentation of a defined outline: a problem to be answered, the theoretical background, the research method, the results, the analysis and the discussions.
