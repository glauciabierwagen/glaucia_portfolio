My history

I am from Brazil. There I grown up and had my first experiences. It is a country with a beautiful and rich culture.

I majored in Pedagogy and worked as a teacher in elementary schools where I learned about the diversity of this big country, but also some dificultities. In the schools, I learned with students and co-workers about solidarity, cooperation, colaborative learning, culture and social critical analysys.

When I was already a teacher I majored in Information Tecnhonolgy, but I never worked as developer. I used the course to understand technologies applied in learning.

I also did academic advanced studies. The scholar space was the spot where I learned to research and constantly develop my autonomy. And, now I've been in Sweden, learning to change my career, intendind to be a developer.
